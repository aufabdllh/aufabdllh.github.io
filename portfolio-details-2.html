About D-STAC

D-STAC is a web-based system developed with a dashboard as a unique feature to ease the management of the student affairs field in the university. The dashboard visualizes the information in the simplest and attractive form via data visualization in a chart and graph. D-STAC can be accessed by the public for a general feature, while student who is registered to this system and admin staff of student affairs have their own privileges to this system. The Agile Methodology has been applied for the development consisting of requirement, design, development, testing, and deployment. Findings from User Acceptance Testing (UAT) show that 86% of respondents are satisfied while 14% of them are very satisfied with the system. This system also does not have a high-vulnerability severity through the conducted penetration testing. Usability testing is conducted after the development phase to evaluate the overall usability of the system by the end user. D-STAC system received positive feedback as an alternative for student association management, specifically on the program announcement feature and dashboard. Moreover, it gives significant impact to the students and staff in university to easily manage the student association information and activity and encourages the university towards digitalization. In short, D-STAC provides a centralized platform for student association with a statistic dashboard as a unique feature that can enhance user engagement which aligns with Shift 1 in Malaysia Education Blueprint to develop the holistic graduates and GIINA model that employs by USIM organization with the principles of human resource development in the Quran and Sunnah which always emphasize the holistic aspect. Besides, it supports the sustainable communities in 17 SDG goals by reducing the use of paper and saving trees. D-STAC can be commercialized for any university organization specifically in the student affairs division to promote sustainable living.
